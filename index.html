To instigate the creation of 10,000 bussiness leaders in Africa over the next ten years

How do we acheive this

We select talented teenagers from across the continent into our training program where we give them mentorship (from established founders who started young, preferably in thier teenage so they can relate), access to our network, media publicity for achievements and possible funding opportunities.

Exclusive to teenagers we don't just offer training we track progress of our young teenage fellows and offer support required along the way.

We say it's okay to fail
At StartUp Front we believe that if we can encourage early failures when there is not much at stake, then we can create a culture that supports risk-takers who can actually change the world from a young age.
